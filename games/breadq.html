Welcome to The Bread Quest, a Low Poly First Person Game With Fun Adventures!

As the owner of a small bread shop, your goal is to defeat the Baguette Lord in a series of challenges. These challenges will take you through a variety of environments, from the inside of a baking oven to the top of a mountain.

You'll need to use your wits and game skills to overcome each obstacle in your way. Along the way, you'll collect a variety of items that will help you on your quest, including loaves of bread that can be used as weapons: because in Bread Quest, bread is mightier than the sword!

Download today, and start your playing!
