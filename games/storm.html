Welcome to StormTides, a menu based open world RPG that will keep you captivated for hours on end. From the moment you begin playing, you'll be enveloped in our mesmerizing game style. And with so much to do in the game, you'll never get bored! One of the things that makes StormTides unique is our attention to detail. Every aspect of the game has been carefully crafted to provide the best possible experience. For example, we've completely reworked the weapon damage system to make it more realistic and balanced. And our armor durability system ensures that your armor will always be up to the task of protecting you in battle. Of course, no RPG would be complete without a vast array of customization options. You'll be able to choose from a wide variety of armor and weapons, and you can even customize your character's appearance to suit your own personal taste. There are two type of this series curently. There is StormTides Unity, which is built in the unity game engine and has higher graphics then the other version. StormTides Byte is the second version. It has been crafted in windows batch script to be optimized to be ran on virtually any computer. So come on in and explore the world of StormTides! We guarantee you won't be disappointed.
